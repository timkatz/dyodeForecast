# DYODE Sales Forecast 2026

Sales forecasting and strategic planning for DYODE, an eCommerce agency specializing in Shopify and BigCommerce development, marketing, and consulting services.

## Repository Contents

- **2026-sales-forecast.md** - Comprehensive sales forecast and strategic plan for 2026
- **client-data.csv** - Master client database tracking engagements, contracts, and revenue
- **CLAUDE.md** - Guidance for AI assistants working with this repository

## Quick Overview

### Current State (Nov 2025)
- **MRR:** $292,946
- **Projected 2025 Revenue:** ~$3.2M
- **Services:** Development (61%), Marketing (29%), Consulting (6%)

### 2026 Goals
- **Target MRR:** $360-370k (38-42% growth)
- **Target Revenue:** ~$4.2M
- **Key Initiative:** Reverse 3-year decline trend from $4.86M peak (2022)

## Strategic Priorities

1. **Maintain Shopify Plus Partner Status** - Close 4-6 Shopify Plus projects
2. **Reduce Churn** - From 35% to 20% through better retention
3. **Systematic Lead Generation** - Martal partnership for consistent SQLs
4. **Contract Mix Shift** - Move to 85% retainer / 15% project revenue
