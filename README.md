## Quick Overview

### Current State (Nov 2025)
- **MRR:** $292,946
- **Projected 2025 Revenue:** ~$3.2M
- **Services:** Development (61%), Marketing (29%), Consulting (6%)

### 2026 Goals
- **Target MRR:** $360-370k (38-42% growth)
- **Target Revenue:** ~$4.2M
- **Key Initiative:** Reverse 3-year decline trend from $4.86M peak (2022)

## Strategic Priorities

1. **Maintain Shopify Plus Partner Status** - Close 4-6 Shopify Plus projects
2. **Cross-Sell Strategy** - Target 8-10 cross-sells ($54-77k MRR) with AM incentive program
3. **Reduce Churn** - From 35% to 20% through better retention
4. **Systematic Lead Generation** - Martal partnership for consistent SQLs
5. **Contract Mix Shift** - Move to 85% retainer / 15% project revenue
